Eco Prowler: Save the Planet

About the Game

In "Eco Prowler," you play as a tiny, eco-friendly creature on a mission to restore balance to a polluted world. Each level focuses on a specific environmental challenge, such as air pollution, plastic waste, and oil spills. Collect Eco Tokens to progress and avoid Pollution Spirits to survive!

How to Play

  • Use the arrow keys or on-screen joystick to move the Prowler.
  • Collect all Eco Tokens to complete the level.
  • Avoid Pollution Spirits and other enemies.
  • Defeat the boss in Level 5 to save the planet!

Game Manual

Controls

  • Arrow Keys: Move the Prowler up, down, left, or right.
  • On-Screen Joystick: Use the joystick on mobile devices to move the Prowler.

Objectives

  • Eco Tokens: Collect all Eco Tokens in each level to progress to the next level.
  • Pollution Spirits: Avoid these enemies to survive. If you touch them, the game restarts.
  • Boss Fight: In Level 5, defeat the Melting Glacier boss to win the game.

Levels

  • Level 1 (Forest): Collect Eco Tokens while avoiding Smog Spirits.
  • Level 2 (Ocean): Collect Eco Tokens while avoiding Plastic Phantoms.
  • Level 3 (City): Collect Eco Tokens while avoiding Oil Ogres.
  • Level 4 (Urban): Collect Eco Tokens while avoiding Waste Wraiths.
  • Level 5 (Arctic): Defeat the Melting Glacier boss to save the planet!

Tips

  • Plan your route to collect Eco Tokens efficiently.
  • Keep an eye on the timer and avoid enemies at all costs.
  • In Level 5, focus on dodging the boss's attacks while collecting the remaining Eco Tokens.
